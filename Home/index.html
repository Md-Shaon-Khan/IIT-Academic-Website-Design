Frequently Asked Questions

What is IIT Academic Website

The IIT Academic Website is a centralized platform for academic resources.

Who can upload the question

Only authorized faculty can upload questions.

Who can upload the Project and Thesis Paper

Students can upload projects and thesis papers after approval.

How to access past questions

Past questions are available by course and semester in the archive.

Can external users view resources

Only registered IIT students and faculty can access the resources.

Is there a search feature

Yes, the website has a smart search and filter for quick access.
